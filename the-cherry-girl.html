TO THE CHERRY GIRL

To the person whom I know little but care for a lot — we both know each other less, yet care deeply.

I lost my words when I started to write a letter to you, maybe because I know less about you, or maybe because I know more about your way.

The weather you love speaks of the freedom you long to explore.

Your presence, like a cherry blossom's fragrance, is beautiful and precious, even if it is only for a short time.

Your kindness, care, and love feel gentle and soft, just like the petals of a cherry blossom.

The rare moments with you are deeply treasured, just like people treasure cherry blossoms, knowing they bloom for only a little while.

You bring happiness and lightness wherever you go, like cherry blossoms in spring.

Beautiful people may not stay forever, so I want to cherish every moment with you, just like one cherishes the blossoms of spring.

To me, you are a cherry blossom —

Brief our knowing, yet endless our care.

Like petals that kiss the spring air,

Your presence feels rare, beyond compare.

Moments with you, though fleeting and few,

Are treasures my heart holds onto true.

You are like the fiercest storm —

Wild rain, roaring thunder, the heavy breath of the skies.

You find beauty in the chaos, strength in the winds,

Yet still, you stand there, thinking you are unworthy to feel it.

As if the storm itself did not rage just to meet you,

As if the sky did not split open just to touch your soul.

But hear me — you deserve to stand beneath every sky,

To admire every wonder this world holds,

For you are more than worthy of its greatest beauty.

Maybe I can't give you the world called Earth,

but I can give you a world of care —

a world of silence, where your secrets rest upon my chest,

a world of love, the kind you seek when you're feeling down,

a world of support, where when you're low and need someone to yap through your day,

I'll be there — listening to every word, smiling at every ramble.

I'll offer my shoulder when your head needs a place to rest,

and my heart will be all ears, always — anytime, every time, just to hear you.

And above all, I'll give you a world of trust, built just for you.

Can you share your spring and sorrow with me for a short period, like your favourite cherry blossom?

Are you sure? Please say yes...

Thank you for sharing this moment with me ❤️
